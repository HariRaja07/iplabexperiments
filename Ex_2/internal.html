FOLDABLE SMARTPHONES

A foldable smartphone (also known as a foldable phone or simply foldable) is a smartphone with a folding form factor. It is a modern flip phone.[1][2] Some variants of the concept use multiple touchscreen panels on a hinge, while other designs utilise a flexible display.

Concepts of such devices date back as early as Nokia's "Morph" concept in 2008, and a concept presented by Samsung Electronics in 2013 (as part of a larger set of concepts utilizing flexible OLED displays), while the first commercially available folding smartphones with OLED displays began to emerge in November 2018.
